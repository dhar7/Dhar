About me

I'm Argha and I'm

I have been serving as a faculty member in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET). Before joining at KUET, I obtained my B.Sc in CSE from KUET in February, 2023.

I am highly enthusiastic about Machine Learning (ML), Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Security. My undergraduate thesis is on Improvement of Vehicular Network Security using Cascaded Machine Learning.

Research interests

Machine Learning with Security

    My research interests fall at the intersection of machine learning and security. Two research directions include: 1. Attacks using ML: Investigating security threats in inversion techniques of a Deep Neural Network, i.e., retrieving the input from a partially computed vector. 2. ML for better security: Using ML systems to develop a robust Intrusion Detection System that emphasizes more on Attack data.

Natural Language processing

    Machine Translation: The main focus is to improve the translation quality of low-resource language (Bangla-English) using neural networks and backtranslation.
